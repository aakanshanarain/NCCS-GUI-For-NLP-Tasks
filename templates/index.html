Machine Learning for Histology Reports
Leverage on the predictive power of machine learning models to make predictions on histology reports. Upload a csv file to the model you want to run, and then click run model.
Malignant tumor prediction
This model predicts whether a reported tumor is malignant or benign.



Primary site of cancer prediction
This model predicts the primary site(s) of cancer from the reports.



Grade of cancer prediction
This model predicts the grade of the cancer present in the reports.



Important: After selecting the model, it may take a while, depending on the size of the csv file, for the model to run. The results page will load once the model is done predicting!
